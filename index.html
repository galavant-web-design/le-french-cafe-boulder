About

Le French Café is a casual French restaurant and café serving breakfast, lunch, and pastries. When you arrive, you'll be greeted with a smile and surrounded by the casual and fun French flare that is Le French Café.

Owned by the French duo, Agnes and Quentin Garrigou, their dream of opening a French restaurant began years ago when they still lived in France. They first realized their dream operating cafes in Miami, before moving west to Boulder where they opened Le French Café in 2017.

Serving French food with a smile.
